Resources

There are hundreds of bird books available. Some cover an entire continent, and some cover a single species.

Obtaining the right bird books will be an invaluable resource for you. Two must-have books would be a book covering the entire country, nd then a book covering your part of the country (Birds of the Northwest, Birds of the Southeast, etc.)

Some books show illustrations, where others show photographs. The matter of preference is yours. Sometimes when it's difficult to figure out which bird it is, several books can help.

Our Philosophy

We have enjoyed watching birds in our backyard for as long as we can remember. To some, this may seem like a boring hobby, but the more you get to know about birds, the more excited you'll get when you see a species for the first time.

We want to give you the tools, resources, and knowledge to enable you to thouroughly enjoy watching backyard birds year round.

Our intent is that we make bird watchers out of anyone who might have a slight interest. We hope you're one of those!
